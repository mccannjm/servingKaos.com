What Zeus Is

Zeus is a writing instrument. You type with brackets. The brackets classify your thinking into rings. A constellation renders the shape of what you're working through — live, as you write.

It runs on your Mac. Metal GPU. No cloud. No account. No data leaves your machine.

The instrument learns you. Not through surveillance. Through the shape of your thinking over time.

The Rings

Six rings. Six colors. Every bracket you place classifies the thought inside it.

What It Looks Like

You write like this. The rings appear in real time.

*I want to understand why this keeps happening*
{The data shows a 40% drop every Tuesday}
[Maybe it's the deploy schedule? Or the cache invalidation?]
^I'm circling the same three hypotheses^
:something connects the deploy timing to the cache:
vvTuesdayDropvv

Each line becomes a node. The constellation shows the shape — what's pulling you, what's grounded, what's still open. The instrument doesn't interpret. It reflects.

The Kernel

Three primitives. That's it. That runs anywhere.

Weighted Graph
Nodes + edges + weight + decay. Things you return to get heavier. Things you abandon fade. Time has a vote.
Ring Classifier
Any input decomposes into Intent, Reality, and Gap. The brackets are the notation. The rings are the classification.
Version Heartbeat
Every mutation increments. Every increment re-renders. The constellation is always current. It breathes with you.

How It Works

You write. The ring classifier runs on every keystroke — microseconds. The parser builds a graph — debounced, 150ms. Metal renders the constellation — 60fps. An optional AI reads the pattern and names what it sees.

The constellation doesn't need a cloud. It needs a GPU and a human typing brackets.

Between sessions, Heimdall guards the gate. Nodes decay with a 2-hour half-life. Pins never decay. What had weight keeps weight. What faded waits in quarantine — say its name and it comes back.

Who This Is For

Writers who think by writing. Builders who think by building. Anyone who's ever had a feeling about where an idea is going but couldn't see the shape of it.

Zeus doesn't tell you what to think. It shows you the shape of what you're already thinking. The rings were always there. The brackets just make them visible.
